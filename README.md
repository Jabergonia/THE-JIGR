# THE-JIGR
An Association of Computing Machinery Techsprint Hackathon Team.

ALAI is an offline AI-powered study companion designed to make learning more accessible, engaging, and personalized for students. Built to support learners through multilingual translation, intelligent tutoring, and academic progress monitoring, ALAI empowers students to learn anytime and anywhere, even without an internet connection. By combining adaptive learning, a friendly AI companion, and data-driven performance insights, ALAI helps students strengthen their understanding, build confidence, and achieve their educational goals.

1. Multilingual Learning & Translation Feature
This feature provides fast and accurate offline translation between English and Filipino, as well as Filipino and selected Philippine dialects, helping students understand lessons in the language they are most comfortable with. Powered by an on-device AI model, students can translate words, phrases, explanations, and educational content even without an internet connection. This promotes inclusive learning for diverse learners and encourages better comprehension across different languages and regional dialects.

2. Adaptive AI Tutor & Quiz Generator
The AI Tutor explains lessons and concepts using the student's preferred language and educational level, ensuring that explanations are clear, age-appropriate, and easy to understand. Whether learning Mathematics, Science, English, or other subjects, students receive personalized guidance tailored to their needs. After each lesson, the AI can automatically generate practice quizzes, review questions, and mini-assessments to reinforce learning and measure comprehension.

3. Friendly Learning Buddy AI
The application features a warm and encouraging AI companion designed to make learning feel engaging, interactive, and less intimidating for students. The AI communicates in a positive and supportive manner, offering praise, encouragement, hints, and study tips throughout the learning process. By creating a friendly learning environment, students are more likely to stay motivated, build confidence, and develop a positive attitude toward studying.

4. Smart Academic Progress Tracker
This feature allows students to record quiz scores, assignment results, examination grades, and other academic achievements in a centralized dashboard. The AI analyzes the collected data to identify strengths, weaknesses, learning trends, and areas that need additional focus. Through visual progress charts and performance indicators, students can easily monitor their growth and stay informed about their academic development.

5. AI Performance Insights & Learning Reports / Summarization 
Using the student's recorded scores and learning activity, the AI generates comprehensive performance summaries that highlight achievements, improvement areas, and study recommendations. The reports provide easy-to-understand insights that help students, parents, and teachers monitor learning progress and make informed decisions. In addition, the AI suggests personalized study plans and targeted practice exercises to help learners continuously improve their academic performance.
